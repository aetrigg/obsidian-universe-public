## üìñ The Series

### Core Concept & Setting
The series is a contemporary romance saga centered on **5th of June (5oJ)**, a fictional boy band from Austin, Texas, that has reached "American One Direction" levels of fame by 2025.

**Origin Story:**  
The band formed in high school as an acoustic pop-punk group. They gained initial notoriety after reaching the Top 48 on America's Got Talent in 2020 via online audition, where they were eliminated for lacking versatility. This failure prompted a pivot to a polished, vocal-heavy pop sound managed by their "sixth member," Ted Dalton.

**The "Junies":**  
The series heavily integrates modern fandom culture. The fans, known as "Junies," have created extensive lore (such as the theory that the band is named after a Battle of the Bands date), and their social media activity often drives the plot.

### Narrative Structure

**The Framing Device:**  
The series utilizes a retrospective framing device similar to *How I Met Your Mother*. The stories are set in or around 2025 (often during an interview), where each member recounts the specific story of how they met their significant other.

**POV:**  
Each book focuses on a different couple, alternating points of view between the band member and his love interest.

**Vibe:** Quiet, romantic, and emotionally observant. He is an adoptee who struggles with anxiety and identity.

**Vibe:** The "baby" of the group (though he comes from a huge family). He is chaotic, bubbly, and radiates "golden retriever" energy.

## üìö Story Summaries (Chronological Release Order)

### Book 1: The Shy One (Jordan & Mac)
Set during the Wild Nights tour in 2023. Jordan meets Mac through her best friend after a concert. Their relationship begins over text and evolves when Mac joins the tour to ghostwrite the band's behind-the-scenes book. The plot focuses on their shared trauma regarding adoption and the challenges of navigating tour life while managing Mac's epilepsy. The climax involves a grand gesture in a sunflower field.

### Book 2: The Responsible One (Nolan & Tiffany)
An enemies-to-lovers story set around 2024. It begins when Tiffany posts a viral TikTok calling Nolan rude. The resulting PR nightmare forces them into proximity, where they realize their public feud masks a genuine connection.

### Book 3: The Heartthrob (Eli & Laura)
Set earlier in the timeline during the Summer Rain tour (2022). Eli sings the song "For Laura" during a concert and asks if anyone named Laura is in the audience. He spots Laura Collins, and later runs into her by chance at a diner, sparking a fairytale "fan-meets-idol" romance.

### Book 4: The Bad Boy (Beck & Autumn)
Set during the band's more mature, darker era (approx. 2025/2026). Beck is spiraling into drug use and burnout. Autumn, who taught him piano when they were kids, re-enters his life after hearing a song he wrote about her. This is a second-chance romance focused on redemption and healing.

### Book 5: The Cute One (Aiden & Delilah)
Set during the peak of the band's global fame. Aiden enters a secret relationship with pop superstar Delilah James. The conflict revolves around two massive celebrities trying to maintain a private love life while under constant media scrutiny.
