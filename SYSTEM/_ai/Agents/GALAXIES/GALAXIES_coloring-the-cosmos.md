The @coloringthecosmos galaxy is Andrea's social media presence focused on cozy coloring content. This galaxy encompasses her Instagram and TikTok accounts where she shares her coloring journey, techniques, marker collections, and the meditative process of bringing illustrations to life.

The account celebrates the calm, creative joy of coloring as a hobby and art form, creating a cozy aesthetic space for fellow coloring enthusiasts.

**Current Status:** This galaxy is on hiatus while Andrea focuses creative energy on the 5th of June and Obsidian galaxies. Andrea was posting consistently to @coloringthecosmos until the 5th of June project returned to her life in 2025, at which point the coloring accounts fell to the wayside. The infrastructure remains in place for when she's ready to resume content creation.
