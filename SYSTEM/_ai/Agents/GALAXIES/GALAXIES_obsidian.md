## üé® Design Philosophy

### Aesthetics First
- Beautiful UI enhances engagement and joy
- Cosmic theming throughout 
- Color-coded nodes, tags, & life areas
- Pixel art banners for visual delight
- Custom CSS for polished experience
- Everything should feel intentional and cohesive

### Function Follows Form (But Both Matter)
- Systems must be both beautiful AND functional
- No compromise on visual design
- Performance matters, but not at the expense of aesthetics
- Front-end development background informs all design decisions

### Work WITH Patterns, Not Against Them
- Systems designed for reactive daily execution
- Automation reduces friction for ADHD workflow
- Captures information at the moment without demanding structure
- Organization happens when ready, not forced upfront
